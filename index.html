Game Settings

Commander's Manual

Objective: Defend your Castle from relentless waves of enemies. If the Castle falls, you lose.

Combat & Counters: Every unit has a damage type and an armor class — there is no luck, only matchups. Watch the wave preview and field the counter:

  • Slash (Militia, Swordsman, Knight) cuts down unarmored mobs, glances off heavy plate.
  • Pierce (Spearman, Archer) punches light armor; arrows bounce off shields. Crossbow bolts ignore armor entirely.
  • Blunt (Paladin, Catapult) crushes shields and heavy armor, wasted on rabble.
  • Magic (Mage, Cleric) burns through armor of every kind.
  • Spearmen skewer large foes (Ogres). Melee can't reach flying Dragons — Archers and Crossbowmen hit them for bonus damage, and your Castle's flak-fire blasts them from range.
  • Gold damage numbers mean a counter hit; gray means resisted — recruit accordingly.

Economy: Build Mines to generate Gold and Iron continuously (each extra mine costs more). Defeating elites yields crystals and additional iron. Recruiting many of the same unit gets progressively more expensive — mixed armies are cheaper and cover each other's weaknesses. Barracks, Archery Range, Academy and War Forge unlock advanced troops each level.

Your Castle: Its battlements scorch anything that reaches the gate with a short-ranged magic blast that shrugs off armor — a last line of defense, not a sniper. Both its firepower and your gold income grow region by region, so later campaigns lean on the castle more than the earliest ones.

War Council & Renown: Clearing campaign regions and surviving endless waves earns Renown, a currency that carries between runs. Spend it in the War Council (main menu) to permanently unlock advanced troops — once unlocked they are ready from the start of every level and endless run, with no building required. You still start each region with Militia and Archers; permanent unlocks widen that opening roster as you progress.

Controls & Camera:

  • Edge Scrolling: Move your mouse to the left/right edges of the screen, or use A/D or Arrow Keys. (On mobile, swipe across the screen).
  • Minimap: Click on the minimap (bottom left) to instantly jump to a location.
  • Auto-Train: Right-Click (or Long-Press on mobile) any unit button to toggle automatic recruitment.
  • Call Wave: Cleared the field early? Hit ⚔ Call Wave Now (or press N) to summon the next wave immediately instead of waiting out the timer.

Commander Spells: Use your Mana to cast devastating spells. Click a spell (or press Z/X/C), then click anywhere on the battlefield.

Research: Open the Tech Tree (Y) to purchase permanent upgrades. Upgrades dramatically improve your odds in later waves.
