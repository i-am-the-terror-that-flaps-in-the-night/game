How to Play

Goal: Defend your castle from waves of enemy stick figures. Don't let them destroy your Castle.

Economy: Build Mines to generate gold. Kill enemies for bounties. Spend gold on units and buildings.

Units: Train melee units to block enemies. Train ranged units for damage. Clerics heal. Knights charge. Mages deal AoE damage.

Buildings: Barracks unlock better melee. Academy unlocks mages. Towers shoot automatically. Walls block enemies.

Tech: Open the Tech Tree (Y) to buy permanent upgrades for your army.

Controls: Mouse to pan camera. Click units to inspect. Hotkeys 1-9 for units, Q-T for buildings. Space to pause.
