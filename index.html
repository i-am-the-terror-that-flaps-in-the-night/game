Game Settings

Commander's Manual

Objective: Defend your Castle from relentless waves of enemies. If the Castle falls, you lose.

Economy: Build Mines to generate Gold continuously. Defeating enemies also yields bounties.

Controls & Camera:

  • Edge Scrolling: Move your mouse to the left/right edges of the screen to pan the camera.
  • Minimap: Click on the minimap (bottom left) to instantly jump to a location.
  • Keyboard: Use Arrow Keys or A/D to pan. Use Hotkeys 1-9 for units, Q-T for buildings, Z/X/C for Spells.

Commander Spells: Use your Mana (regenerates over time) to cast devastating spells. Click a spell (or press Z/X/C), then click anywhere on the battlefield to cast.

Research: Open the Tech Tree (Y) to purchase permanent upgrades using Gold. Upgrades dramatically improve your odds in later waves.
